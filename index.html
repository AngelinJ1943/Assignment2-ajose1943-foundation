Nelson Mandela

Biography

Nelson Mandela was born in 1918 in South Africa. He played a crucial role in the fight against apartheid, spending 27 years in prison before becoming the first black president of South Africa in 1994.

Quote

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
- Nelson Mandela
